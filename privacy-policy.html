Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the JM Labs website and to software products published by JM Labs, including Bullion Master. Some products may have additional product-specific workflows or disclosures depending on how they operate.

2. Website information

When you visit the JM Labs website, standard technical information such as browser, device, and usage context may be processed for site delivery, security, and basic performance monitoring.

3. Product information

JM Labs products may process business and account information needed to operate their workflows. In Bullion Master, this can include local records such as ledger data, stock context, reports, settings, attachments, account identifiers, and related operational metadata required for the product to function.

4. Local-first handling

Bullion Master is designed around local-first business operation. Most day-to-day working records are expected to remain on the user device unless the user chooses features such as authentication, backup, restore, sync, subscription validation, export, or sharing.

5. Why data may be used

  • To operate product features requested by the user.
  • To support authentication, device integrity, and subscription state.
  • To provide backup, restore, export, sync, or sharing flows.
  • To maintain security, service reliability, and abuse prevention.

6. Permissions and access

  • Internet access may be used for sign-in, backup, restore, sync, and validation flows.
  • Camera, image, or file access may be used when a user attaches records or exports files.
  • Storage access may be used for local backups, local records, and user-initiated exports.

7. Sharing and disclosure

JM Labs does not sell business data. Information may be shared only where necessary for service operation, user-requested actions, legal compliance, fraud prevention, or protection of platform integrity.

8. Retention

Retention depends on the product workflow and user action. Local records may remain on the device until edited, deleted, overwritten, wiped, or reset by the user. Cloud-related data retention may depend on the enabled feature and service state.

9. User responsibility

Users remain responsible for the information they enter, for verifying their own outputs, and for maintaining secure access to their device, credentials, PINs, and any backup or recovery details tied to product use.

10. Updates

This Privacy Policy may change as the JM Labs website or products evolve. The latest published version on this site will be the governing version.
