01

System Overview

Bullion Master is engineered as a local-first application. This means the primary source of truth for your data resides exclusively on your device hardware.

Cloud infrastructure is utilized only as a secondary utility for synchronization across authorized devices and secure off-site backup. We do not maintain unencrypted access to your vault contents on our servers.

04

Cloud Backup Architecture

Zero-Knowledge Encryption

Backups are automatically encrypted before leaving the device. The encryption keys are derived directly from your master password and biometric salts. JM Labs does not possess the keys required to decrypt your cloud backups. If you lose your master password, your cloud-stored data is unrecoverable.

06. Information Usage

Data is processed solely for delivering core features: executing transactions, authenticating user sessions, and facilitating automated encrypted backups. No behavioral tracking is implemented.

07. Required Permissions

  • Network: Cloud sync and backup operations.
  • Camera: QR code scanning and document capture.
  • Filesystem: Storing encrypted local databases.

08. Data Sharing

JM Labs does not sell, trade, or rent your data to any third party. We are a zero-monetization infrastructure for user data.

09. Retention & Erasure

You may initiate a full "Nuclear Wipe" flow at any time, which purges all local data and instructs our servers to delete your associated encrypted backup shards.

10. User Controls

Full control over your data environment via the Settings terminal. Manage ledger edits, backup frequency, and local storage limits directly.

11. Policy Updates

Significant changes to this protocol will be communicated via in-app terminal broadcast. Continued use after such notification constitutes protocol acceptance.
