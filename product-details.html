Module Breakdown

Engineered for Total Bullion Control

Explore the professional modules of Bullion Master. Built to merge financial accuracy with specialized bazaar workflows.

1. Command Center

  • Instant view of total Metal + Cash receivables/payables.
  • Real-time Physical Stock in hand on your main screen.
  • Live P&L updates calculated against market rates.
  • Quick Lock feature for secure workstation management.

2. Digital Party Ledger

  • Dedicated party profiles with automatic opening balances.
  • Quick buttons for Buy, Sell, Payment, and Delivery entries.
  • Instant Closing Balance updates as you post entries.
  • Full audit trail for deep historical ledger review.

3. Daily Bazaar Ledger

  • Unified list of every transaction recorded today.
  • Advanced filtering by Party, Date, or Metal Type.
  • High-speed entry interface for busy trading hours.
  • Clear separation of Cash Adjustments and Metal flows.

4. Stock Monitoring

  • Master tracking for Gold, Silver, and custom alloys.
  • Real-time "Overbought" or "Oversold" position alerts.
  • Manual stock reconciliation with detailed audit logs.
  • In-hand inventory analysis across various purity levels.

5. Refinery & Old Gold

  • Badla Swaps: Easy tracking of metal-to-metal exchanges.
  • Refinery Desk: End-to-end Old Gold to Fine Gold tracking.
  • Capture melting loss/gain for every batch processed.
  • Auto-calculate settlement charges in metal or cash.

6. One-Click Reports

  • Generate Sales, Purchase, and Turnover reports instantly.
  • Professional PDF/Excel exports for accountants.
  • Pending Position summaries grouped by party name.
  • Automatic Salary and Expense tracking reports.
